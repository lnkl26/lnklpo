About Me

Artist & Small Business Owner

Hello! I'm Chiara, but I'm known as Kaino to my friends and art pals. I am a traditional and digital artist, as well as a painter and a small business owner. I've been drawing ever since I can remember and I hope that one day it will not only be my hobby, but also my job. Art can change the world, and I'd love to be part of that change.
